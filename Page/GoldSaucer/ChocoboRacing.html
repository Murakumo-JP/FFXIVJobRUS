Chocobo racing is an exciting competition at the Gold Saucer that makes use of specially trained chocobos.
From improving their abilities through rigorous training to breeding retired chocobos for their traits, you can raise the champion chocobo you've always wanted.

Requirements

  • Katering
  • Players must first complete the quest “So You Want to Be a Jockey."

Getting Started

Where to Participate

To acquire a racing chocobo, register your chocobo, and begin racing, players must first proceed to Chocobo Square by speaking with the lift operator Saethrith at the Entrance Square (X:4.4 Y:6.7).
